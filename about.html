Giulia Frani is an Italian-born actor and playwright shaping a creative life between the UK and Italy.
Her work lives at the intersection of language, culture, and performance — bold, borderless, and driven by story.
Alongside acting, Giulia is an emerging playwright whose work explores human connection, identity, and the spaces between cultures.

Giulia began her training in 2016 at the Film Academy (I.F.A.) in Pescara, studying acting for camera and stage under industry professionals including Zulima Memba, Marco Cassini, Daniele Ciglia, and Ludovico Fremont.
In 2019, Giulia moved to London to deepen her craft, attending short courses at The Unseen with Arjun Rose and the London Actors Workshop with Daniel Dresner and Jon Sidgwick.
